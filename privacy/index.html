Privacy Policy – Decades: The Music Quiz

Last updated: September 22, 2025

Cecado AB (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the mobile application Decades: The Music Quiz (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device and diagnostics data: device model, operating system version, language, app version, and crash logs for troubleshooting and improving stability.
  • Usage data: basic interactions and in-app events that help us understand feature usage and performance.
  • Gameplay data: scores, progress, settings and similar data, typically stored locally on your device.
  • Support communications: information you provide if you contact us for help.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of data (such as financial information, health data, or government IDs).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve App functionality and performance.
  • To diagnose and fix bugs and crashes.
  • To analyze aggregated usage to improve features and user experience.
  • To respond to your requests and provide support.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers that process data on our behalf (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, hosting) under appropriate confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal reasons where we are required to do so to comply with applicable law or lawful requests.

4. Data Retention

  • Gameplay data on your device is removed when you uninstall the App (unless you separately back it up).
  • Diagnostics/analytics data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please use the contact information below.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we implement appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy at cecado dot com.
